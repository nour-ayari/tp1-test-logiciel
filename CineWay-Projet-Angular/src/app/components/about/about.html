Our Story

CineWay was born from a simple idea: movie lovers should spend less time booking tickets and more time enjoying the magic of cinema. Founded in 2020, we started as a small team passionate about both technology and film.

What began as a weekend project has grown into a comprehensive platform that serves hundreds of thousands of movie enthusiasts. We've partnered with cinemas across the country to bring you the best selection of films, from blockbuster releases to indie gems.

Today, CineWay continues to innovate, introducing features like personalized recommendations, real-time notifications, and seamless mobile booking to make your movie-going experience unforgettable.
